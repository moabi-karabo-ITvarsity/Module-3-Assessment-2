Mushroom Dumplings in Toasted Ginger and Garlic Broth

500ml bowl - R55,00

Make-ahead mushroom duxelles makes a rich filling for these tender, satisfying dumplings.The broth, infused with toasted ginger and garlic,gets an extra layer of rich mushroom flavour from dried white flower shiitake mushrooms, which have a bolder flavour than regular dried shiitakes, which are a fine substitute.
